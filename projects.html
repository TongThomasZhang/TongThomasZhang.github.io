Projects

Trading

Trading Bitcoin Using Momentum and Reddit Sentiment (2019): In this project my partner and I analyze the BTC price rollercoaster of 2018. We reasonably hypothesized that price fluctuations were driven mainly by sentiment, so we used traditional Momentum and Reddit Sentiment trading strategies and compared them to holding.

Time Series Momentum for Small Cap Stock Prediction (2020): In this exploratory data analysis we find that, although Time Series Momentum (TSM), has been found somewhat useless with Large Cap stocks in recent years, It has been relatively robust across Small Cap stocks. Particularly in the 1990-2000s era.

LSTM for Small Cap Stock Prediction (2020): With the advent of neural nets, we look at the effect of using the LSTM (Long Short Term Memory) to predict time series stock data.

Stock Trading usinng Naive ML Models (2018): An exploration of applying basic machine learning models.

Car Pricing on Craigslist If you list a car on Craigslist, what's the right price? This project explores car price listing on craigslist and will give you a price estimate for your car given existing data.
